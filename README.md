**VitaTrack AI** is an intelligent fitness AI bot built to support users in maintaining a healthier and more consistent lifestyle.
It acts as a smart digital fitness companion that helps guide daily health habits, encourages consistency, and provides helpful insights related to fitness and wellbeing.

The bot is designed with the idea that fitness should be simple, motivating, and accessible to everyone. Instead of complex tracking systems, VitaTrack AI focuses on creating a smooth and interactive experience where users can engage with an AI assistant that understands their fitness journey and supports them along the way.

Whether someone is starting their fitness journey or trying to stay disciplined with their routine, VitaTrack AI aims to provide a reliable companion that promotes better habits, awareness, and motivation through intelligent responses and assistance.

This project reflects an interest in building practical AI-driven solutions that solve real-life problems and improve everyday routines through technology.
